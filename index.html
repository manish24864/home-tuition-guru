Frequently Asked Questions

What is Home Tuition Guru?

Home Tuition Guru is a platform connecting students and parents with qualified home tutors across Nepal. We aim to make it easy to find the perfect tutor for your academic needs.

How do I find a tutor?

As a student or parent, you can post a tuition vacancy detailing your requirements (subject, grade, location, etc.). Qualified teachers will then apply to your vacancy, and you can review their profiles and select the best fit.

How do I become a tutor?

Teachers can create a detailed profile showcasing their expertise, subjects, experience, and availability. You can then browse and apply to student vacancies that match your skills. Once selected, you can connect with the student/parent.

Is there a fee for using the platform?

Our platform aims to provide seamless connections. Specific details regarding any service fees or subscription models will be clearly communicated during the matching process.

How does the matching process work?

Once a student/parent posts a vacancy, interested teachers apply. The student/parent reviews teacher profiles and makes a selection. After confirmation, both parties receive each other's contact details to arrange tuition.
